Do COVID Vaccines Work?

From data cleaning to data mining of Our World in Data covid datasets. Looked at max cases and deaths per country, with the United States leading infected cases but Europe leading deaths.
With the Dashboard, we can see that the death rate sat at 2.16% during July 2021, and we can see the infected curve starting to flatten out after vaccine roll out.

Why Data Analysis?
