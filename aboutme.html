About Me

I have spent the past 2 years doing number crunching and database management from data warehouses for an energy company, and before that I managed financial databases for an Estate Lawyer for 3 years. I am keen to continue developing my career in the field of data analysis. I have always been a numbers person, with exceptional mathematics and computer skills. I am fluent in a number of data management systems and software, including STATA and Excel. And I am currently learning Python, R, Git, HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT and SQL (I made this website btw!). Statistical significance, forecasting, and data-driven optimization are the rhythm of the drumbeat I march to. That love stems directly to my Masters degree of Applied Economics and Predictive Analytics.


On a personal level, I am detail-oriented, organized, and precise in my work. At all my positions, I was allowed the freedom to manage myself, and my work is still all completed in a timely manner. I have strong communication skills with a knack for clear and illuminating presentations, using Tableau, PowerPoint, Pandas, or even just Excel. I am comfortable on my own facing the numbers, but I really enjoy being part of a motivated team of intellectuals.


As the late great Stan Lee said, “I’m happiest when I’m working. If I’m not working, I feel like I’m wasting my time.”
